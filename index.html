Our Story

Wander With Food was ideated back in 2019, with an idea of helping people discover restaurants by their preferences and not just by names. We want to bring the focus back to the food, the experience, and the journey of dining.

Partner with Coimbatore's curated food guide

Why Partner with Us?

Wander with Food is positioned as a Travel Food Guide and therefore, not all the restaurants are onboarded. We hand pick the best dines in Coimbatore to give our customers a memorable dining experience.

"Not every restaurant makes it. Only the best dining experiences are featured on Wander With Food."
