Est. 2019

7 years into building

Wander With Food was ideated back in 2019, with an idea of helping people discover restaurants by their preferences. We curated the restaurants based on type, budget, location and quality. Now our application has grown into an enterprise level platform with over 8+ prominent features. Crafted for the food enthusiasts, the application is all set to go live in and around Coimbatore.

Because you are chosen!

Wander with Food is positioned as a Travel Food Guide and therefore, not all the restaurants are onboarded. We hand pick the best dines in Coimbatore to give our customers a memorable dining experience.

"Not every restaurant makes it. Only the best dining experiences are featured on Wander With Food."
